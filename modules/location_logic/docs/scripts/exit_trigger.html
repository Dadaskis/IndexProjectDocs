A trigger area that handles exit points of locations.
Can be locked/unlocked and provides visual feedback. Handles both locked and unlocked states.

Seriously outdated, untested. I made it at first under influence of Cruelty Squad.
And now? Now it doesn't seem to make any sense anymore. You can do similar
stuff with just simple triggers and STs
