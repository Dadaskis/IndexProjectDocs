Module: sound

Script: sound

Class Name: Sound

Extends: Node

AUTOLOAD

Page last updated:
November 4, 2025 at 21:09 UTC


This singleton manages all audio playback and effects in the game.
It handles:
- Playing 2D/3D sounds with various parameters (volume, pitch, position)
- Environmental audio effects (reverb, delay, underwater effects)
- Sound type categorization (music, voice lines, environmental sounds)
- Volume control and bus routing for different sound types
- Special audio effects (flashbang, underwater filtering)
- Automatic cleanup of finished sounds
- Dynamic audio processing based on game state and environment

The system supports spatial audio, environmental effects, and complex
audio mixing between different sound categories.
