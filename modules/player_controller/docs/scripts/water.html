Module: player_controller

Script: water

Class Name: PlayerControllerWater

Extends: Spatial

NODE

Page last updated:
November 21, 2025 at 23:07 UTC


Water volume system for player and physics interactions
Handles:
- Water surface visualization with dynamic materials/textures
- Buoyancy physics for rigidbodies/characters
- Splash effects and sound triggers
- Underwater rendering effects
- Editor tools for water volume setup
- Water quality settings adaptation
