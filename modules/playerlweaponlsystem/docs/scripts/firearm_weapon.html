Module: player_weapon_system

Script: firearm_weapon

Class Name: PlayerFirearmWeapon

Extends: PlayerWeapon

RESOURCE

Page last updated:
October 23, 2025 at 17:51 UTC


General class for all player firearms that handles:
- Shooting mechanics (single/auto fire modes)
- Ammo management and reloading
- Recoil and weapon sway
- Grenade launcher functionality (if equipped)
- HUD updates (ammo display, crosshair, etc.)
- Checkpoint saving/loading of weapon state
- Perk and difficulty modifications
