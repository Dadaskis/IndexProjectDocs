Description

Guess what it does? Name sounds nice, you know, "Room Debug". Debugging a room. Why would you debug a room? It's not like I'm your mom to mock you for the mess that you may or may not have. And rooms in Purger don't really have any special properties. Alright, I'm not gonna fool around with your mind now. This one is simple, really, RoomDebug is supposed to... debug rooms... Okay, it's getting tricky. There's "Portal-based Occlusion Culling" in this amazing engine, right? It is shaping the space into "rooms" that are "seeing" each-other through "portals". So, these two fuckers can ultimately turns your life into hell especially when you have to analyze why occlusion culling suddenly broke in one of locations. That's the ultimate reason why this class exists.



Usage Example

RoomDebug.debug_enable()
RoomDebug.debug_disable()
RoomDebug.debug_toggle()

It's just a fancy ass singleton.
