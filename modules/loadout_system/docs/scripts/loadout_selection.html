Module: loadout_system

Script: loadout_selection

Class Name: LoadoutSelection

Extends: Camera

NODE

Page last updated:
October 23, 2025 at 17:51 UTC


Handles the 3D loadout selection screen where players can:
- Choose weapons, armor and equipment
- Manage character loadouts while looking from the specific point of view
- Handle deployment
- Display mission briefings and operation info
