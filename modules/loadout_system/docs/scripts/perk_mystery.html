MysteryPerk - Implements a special perk that activates various gameplay-altering effects
based on the player's current loadout configuration. Handles environmental changes,
enemy behavior modifications, visual effects, and special game modes when specific
weapon/gear combinations are equipped. Creates unique gameplay experiences through
dynamic world transformations and rule changes.
