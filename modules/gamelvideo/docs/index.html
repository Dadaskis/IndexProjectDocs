Game video module. I had plans for it, but it wasn't that useful because videos are heavy especially you want to keep a good resolution.

Usage example

GameVideo.play_video( ["game_start_vid.ogv"] )
# Array of videos will play one after another, allowing you to split a huge video
yield(GameVideo, "on_finish")
# Continue logic after finished video
