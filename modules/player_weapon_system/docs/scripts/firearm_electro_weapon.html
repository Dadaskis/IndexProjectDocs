A specialized electrical weapon that creates an
electrical explosion on impact. Extends base firearm functionality with
area-of-effect electrical damage and unique visual effects.

Function

on_firearm_hit

damagable: Damagable, origin: Vector3, shoot_dir: Vector3

void

Called when the weapon hits a damagable target or surface
damagable: The target that was hit (can be null for surface hits)
origin: World position where shot originated from
shoot_dir: Normalized direction vector of the shot
