Module: explosion

Script: explosion

Class Name: Explosion

Extends: Spatial

NODE

Page last updated:
October 23, 2025 at 17:51 UTC


Handles all explosion effects and damage calculations
Manages particles, sounds, physics forces, damage application, and environmental effects
for explosions in the game world. Handles interactions with characters, props, and ragdolls.
