Description

Music singleton... plays music. Hella mindblowing, right? A music player. Nice. It can just play music, it can also do some transitions. It can do some other tricks too. Potentially. But the thing is? Most likely you'd never need anything besides slow play/stop functions, but...

Usage Example

Music.play("m_day1_1_start.ogg")
Music.stop_all()
Music.play_slow("m_day1_1_wait.ogg")
Music.stop_slow_all()
Music.transition("stage0_transition0.wav", "stage0_loop0.wav")

ST_Music might cover all your needs for level design music control.
