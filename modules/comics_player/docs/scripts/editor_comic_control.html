Page last updated:

The puppet master behind the editor comic strip magic - breathes life into shitty panels
with shader sorcery and dynamic text wizardry. Controls:
- Pattern templates
- Picture placement
- Text box generation

Uses shader materials to dynamically transform plain ColorRects into
comic panels, complete with the image controls and scaling voodoo.
Text boxes are spawned like demonic spirits, bound to their speech data.

Not just an editor comic control script - a full-blown comic dimension manipulator.
