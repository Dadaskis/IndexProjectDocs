Module: viewmodel

Script: preloader

Class Name: ViewmodelPreloader

Extends: Node

AUTOLOAD

Page last updated:
October 23, 2025 at 21:10 UTC


Handles preloading and management of viewmodel assets including:
- Weapon models
- Hand models
- Particle effects
- Left hand animations
Provides centralized access to viewmodel resources with performance optimizations
